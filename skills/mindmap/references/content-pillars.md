# LinkedIn Content Pillar Taxonomy

## Usage

This taxonomy organizes 50+ LinkedIn post themes into 14 pillars. Each theme includes:
- **Description**: What the theme covers
- **Example angle**: A specific post idea pattern
- **Context requirement**: Conditions that must be met (or "Universal" if always applicable)

During idea generation, skip themes whose context requirements are not met based on the founder's profile and interview responses.

---

## Pillar 1: Founding Story & Personal Narrative

**Context**: Universal — every founder has a story

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Origin story | How and why the company started | "The email that made me quit my job and start building" | Universal |
| Pivot story | Major direction changes | "We threw away 6 months of work. Here's why it was the best decision." | Has pivoted |
| Early days | The messy beginnings | "Our first office was my kitchen table. Our first customer found us by accident." | Universal |
| Rejection story | Being told no | "47 investors said no. Number 48 changed everything." | Has fundraised |
| Sacrifice story | What they gave up | "I took a 70% pay cut to start this. Here's what I didn't expect." | Universal |
| Imposter syndrome | Feeling unqualified | "I'm a first-time founder and I still Google basic things" | Comfortable with vulnerability |

## Pillar 2: Spiky POVs & Contrarian Takes

**Context**: Universal — strongest pillar for engagement

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Myth-busting | Challenge conventional wisdom | "Everyone says [common advice]. That's wrong, and here's why." | Has strong industry opinions |
| Unpopular opinions | Takes most people disagree with | "Unpopular opinion: [thing everyone does] is destroying [outcome]" | Has contrarian views |
| Hot takes on trends | React to what's trending | "[Hot trend] isn't the future. Here's what is." | Follows industry trends |
| Counter-narrative | Go against the dominant story | "The real reason [big company] succeeded isn't what you think" | Has insider knowledge |
| Competitive commentary | Respectful industry positioning | "There are 50+ tools in our space. Here's how I think about the landscape." | Comfortable discussing competitors |
| Response posts | React to viral takes | "I saw [person]'s post about [topic]. Here's what they're missing." | Active on LinkedIn |

## Pillar 3: Building in Public / Metrics Transparency

**Context**: Requires comfort sharing numbers

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Revenue milestones | Sharing financial progress | "We just hit $[X] MRR. Here's exactly how we got here." | Willing to share revenue |
| Quarterly updates | Regular progress reports | "Q1 recap: what worked, what didn't, what's next" | Has been operating 6+ months |
| Churn transparency | Sharing failures with data | "We lost 15% of customers last month. Here's what we're doing about it." | Has customers + comfortable sharing |
| Pricing decisions | How and why pricing changed | "We raised prices 40%. Here's what happened next." | Has changed pricing |
| Product metrics | Usage and engagement data | "Our most-used feature isn't the one we thought it would be" | Has product data |
| Vanity vs. real metrics | Which numbers actually matter | "Stop tracking [vanity metric]. Here's the only number that matters." | Data-driven founder |

## Pillar 4: Lessons & Hard-Won Wisdom

**Context**: Universal — every founder has lessons

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Expensive mistakes | Lessons that cost money/time | "That $50K mistake taught me more than my MBA" | Universal |
| Retrospectives | Looking back on decisions | "1 year ago I made this decision. Here's what happened." | Has been building 6+ months |
| Pre-founder lessons | Career lessons that transfer | "The best thing my corporate job taught me about startups" | Had career before founding |
| Customer-taught lessons | What users revealed | "A customer call last week completely changed our roadmap" | Has customers |
| Book/resource lessons | Applying wisdom with context | "I re-read [book] as a founder. It hits completely different." | Reads/learns actively |
| Hiring mistakes | Lessons from people decisions | "The hire that almost killed the company" | Has hired (team > 1) |

## Pillar 5: Tactical & Educational Content

**Context**: Best for founders with proven expertise

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Frameworks | Reusable thinking tools | "The 3-question framework I use for every major decision" | Has developed frameworks |
| Process breakdowns | Step-by-step how they do things | "Exactly how I run customer discovery calls (with script)" | Has repeatable processes |
| Tool stacks | What they use and why | "Our entire tech stack costs $200/month. Here's every tool." | Universal |
| Playbooks | Comprehensive guides | "The exact outbound playbook that books 15 meetings/week" | Has proven playbook |
| Data & benchmarks | Industry data with analysis | "We analyzed 1,000 [things]. Here's what we found." | Has data to share |
| ICP lessons | Who to sell to (and who not to) | "We stopped selling to enterprises. Revenue went UP." | Has refined ICP |

## Pillar 6: Industry & Market Commentary

**Context**: Best for founders with deep domain expertise

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Predictions | Where the market is heading | "3 things that will be true about [industry] in 2 years" | Has industry expertise |
| Trend reactions | Responding to market shifts | "[Major announcement] just changed everything for [industry]" | Follows industry closely |
| Market analysis | Breaking down market dynamics | "The [industry] market is consolidating. Here's who survives." | Has market knowledge |
| "Industry is broken" | Calling out systemic issues | "[Industry] has a dirty secret nobody talks about" | Has insider perspective |
| Conference takes | Thoughts from events | "I just got back from [conference]. The vibe has shifted." | Attends events |
| Newsjacking | Reacting to industry news | "[Company] just [did thing]. Here's what it means for founders." | Follows news |

## Pillar 7: Product & Vision

**Context**: Universal for product-focused founders

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Product philosophy | Why the product is built this way | "We intentionally DON'T build [feature]. Here's why." | Has product opinions |
| Feature launches | Sharing what's new with context | "We just shipped [feature]. The story behind why it took 6 months." | Has launched features |
| Vision posts | Where the company is going | "In 5 years, [industry] will look completely different. We're building for that." | Has clear vision |
| Problem statements | Why the problem matters | "This problem costs businesses $[X]B/year. Nobody's fixing it right." | Has defined problem |
| Build vs. buy | Technical/product decisions | "We built our own [thing] instead of using [popular tool]. Here's why." | Has made build/buy decisions |

## Pillar 8: Customer & Social Proof

**Context**: Requires having customers

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Customer stories | Success narratives | "[Customer] went from [before] to [after] in [time]" | Has customer success stories |
| Sales insights | What you learn from selling | "The objection that made us rebuild our entire pitch" | Is actively selling |
| User milestones | Customer achievements | "One of our users just [achieved milestone] using our product" | Has engaged users |
| Objection handling | Public responses to doubts | "People always ask: 'Why wouldn't I just use [competitor]?' Here's my honest answer." | Comfortable addressing objections |

## Pillar 9: Team, Culture & Hiring

**Context**: Requires team size > 1

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Hiring philosophy | How they think about talent | "I don't hire for 'culture fit.' Here's what I look for instead." | Has hired |
| First hire story | The decision to bring someone on | "Hiring employee #1 was the scariest decision I've made as a founder" | Has made first hire |
| Team spotlights | Showcasing team members | "Meet [person]. They turned down [big company] to join us. Here's why." | Has team |

| Remote/async ops | How distributed teams work | "We're fully remote with zero recurring meetings. Here's how." | Is remote |
| Offsite/team stories | Behind-the-scenes team moments | "We just had our first team offsite. The exercise that changed everything." | Has done offsites |

## Pillar 11: Decision-Making & Leadership

**Context**: Universal

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Hardest decisions | The toughest calls | "The hardest decision I made this year (and how I made it)" | Universal |
| Decision frameworks | How they decide | "I make every big decision by asking these 3 questions" | Has frameworks |
| Burnout & mental health | Founder wellbeing | "I burned out last year. Here are the warning signs I missed." | Comfortable sharing |
| Time management | How they spend their time | "I tracked every hour for a month. The results shocked me." | Has thought about this |
| Leadership lessons | Growing as a leader | "The management advice that changed how I lead" | Has team or has grown |

## Pillar 12: Authenticity & Behind-the-Scenes

**Context**: Requires comfort with transparency

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Polished vs. reality | What it's really like | "My LinkedIn vs. my actual Tuesday at 3pm" | Comfortable with vulnerability |
| Day-in-the-life | Typical routines | "A day in the life of a [stage] founder (it's not glamorous)" | Universal |
| Behind-the-scenes | How things actually work | "Here's what our 'overnight success' actually looked like" | Has been building a while |
| "What no one tells you" | Founder surprises | "Nobody told me that being a founder means [unexpected thing]" | Universal |

## Pillar 13: Community & Engagement

**Context**: Universal — good for building audience

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Polls & questions | Engaging the audience | "Quick poll: [binary choice relevant to their industry]" | Universal |
| Shoutouts | Celebrating others | "3 founders building incredible things that nobody's talking about" | Has a network |
| Network activation | Leveraging connections | "I asked 50 founders [question]. The answers were surprising." | Has founder network |
| Industry humor | Relatable memes/jokes | "[Industry]-specific humor that only [role] will understand" | Has sense of humor |

**Context**: Requires comfort sharing personal side

| Theme | Description | Example Angle | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Influences | Who/what shaped them | "The 3 people who shaped how I think about building companies" | Has clear influences |
| Skills journey | Learning new things | "I'm a technical founder learning sales. Week 12 update." | Is actively learning |
